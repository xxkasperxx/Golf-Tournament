PUERTO VALLARTA PRO AM 2013

         PUERTO VALLARTA, MEXICO

Dates:   December 2 - 7 (Tournament 3 - 6 )

 

Entry Fee:   No Charge - Professional

$395.00 - Professional: Spouse/Companion

$2025.00 - Amateurs

$495.00 - Spouse/Companion

* Hotel May Not Be Separated

* Refunds will be in accordance to the Cancellation Policy

 

Includes:   Prizes, Greenfees, Carts, Hotel (5 nights), 3 Night Functions, Ground Transfers,

Club Storage, Range Balls, Tee Prizes & On-Course Beverages

 

Deadline, Payments & Deposits:

 

October 1 or the first 100 Teams.

 

 A Team position can be reserved with a $1000 deposit. The Team balance is due on or before October 1, 2013. All deposit refunds after October 1 will be in accordance to the Cancellation Policy as defined in Event Information. The first 80 Team deposits received will be hosted at the Marriott Casa Magna. The remaining 20 Teams will be assigned to the Westin Regina.  December 2 - 7 are included in the tournament package.

 

Format:   5 Man Teams - 1 professional & 4 Amateurs. Tournament will be a Low Gross and a Low Net, combined total on 2 separate balls. The Tournament will be Pre-Flighted into 4 separate flights. Each flight will have a specific rotation of courses consisting of 3 morning rounds and 1 afternoon round throughout the 72-hole competition. Prizes will be awarded within each flight. All ties for Tournament results will be broken by posted “Tie Breaker.”.  All professionals will be eligible to compete in either a Senior or Open professional competition against other professionals based on a 72-hole aggregate total.

ALL US CITIZENS WILL REQUIRE A PASSPORT when traveling to Mexico. Passports processing are currently running about 3-4 weeks. Passports may be expedited if required.

Early Arrivals/Late Departures and Hotel upgrades: Requests may be made during registration. Both upgrades and "pre" and "post" hotel room nights will be charged directly to the individual at checkout. Early Arrivals/Late Departures hotel will be charged at a special tournament rate of $150.00+ tax per night.

Spouse/Companion Tournament: A 36-hole Spouse/Companion Tournament will be held Dec. 4 & 5 alternating courses at Vista Vallarta. Sign-up will be available at the Welcome Cocktail on Dec. 2. The Entry Fee will be $150.00 and includes prizes and transportation.

 

Player's Pool & Pro / Scratch Registration:  Sign-up for both the Player's Pool and the Pro / Scratch will be available at the Welcome Cocktail Party on December 2.  The Player's Pool is $500 per team and the Pro / Scratch is $200 per 2-man team.  Prize breakdown and distribution information for both will be included in the Tournament Information package provided at hotel check-in.

 

Senior Tees: The professional and amateur Senior Tees option will continue for 2013. Complete details related to "Senior Tees" will be provided in the Tournament Information.

 

Equitable Competition: The Tournament Committee continues to strive in providing competitive equity. To fulfill our goal of competitive integrity, we will continue with Markers assigned to leading Teams per flights & the Modified Pyramid Handicap System to monitor and adjust handicaps round to round. The Tournament Committee will continue to police handicaps (both before and during the event), monitor teams and restrict invitations.

 

Tour Information: Additional Tour information and sign-up will be available during the Welcome Cocktail and afterwards at the Club from Dec.3-6. These Tours have been designed with our Tournament schedule in mind.

 

Practice Round: 8:00 AM and 1:30PM shotgun for the practice round on Dec. 2 will be available to only 28 Teams per course, morning and afternoon (28 @ Marina, 28 @ Nicklaus and 28 @ Weiskopf). Only one practice round is included in the Tournament package and must be used on Monday.  Additional practice rounds are permitted, space available.  Request for specific times, course preference & additional rounds can be made on the "Contact Us" page.  Discounted rates for additional rounds have been arranged for the amateurs: $99.00 @ Marina Vallarta and $125.00 @ Vista Vallarta. Professionals will be charged a flat rate of $50.00 per extra round.

 

Cancellation Policy:

All deposits are fully refundable through October 1, 2013. Refunds October 2 - October 31 will be less 1-night hotel cost + transportation cost per team member (5x).  Refunds beginning November 1 - November 25, will be less 3-night hotel cost + transportation cost per team member (5x).  November 26 - forward, there will be no refund.  We suggest you insure you trip. There are many internet based insurers such as www.insuremytrip.com.  Participants recognize that participation in the Tournament is based on one Professional and 4 Amateurs comprising a team (Team).  IPA has provided individual registration only as a service to facilitate Team entry.  Participants accept that the Cancellation Policy, as defined above will be enforced on a Team base.

Rainout or Inclement weather Policy: Weather patterns for Puerto Vallarta normally does not produce rain in December.  Rains are very seasonal beginning in June and ending in mid-October.  However, in the event of a rainout or inclement weather, the Tournament Committee reserves the right to modify the tournament in any manor it deems fitting to provide the most equitable competition.  Participants understand and acknowledge that the Tournament Entry Fee is for the event in total whether modified or completed in whole.
