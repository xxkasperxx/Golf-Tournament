PUERTO VALLARTA PRO AM 2013

         PUERTO VALLARTA, MEXICO

For 24 years, The Puerto Vallarta Pro Am has been about uniting friends and introducing new acquaintances all in an exotic competitive tournament destination. The Pro Am promises equitable competition, great venues and good times that have become its expected standard. The tournament continues to be about value: 4 tournament rounds + 1 practice round on 3 championship golf courses, individual hotel accommodations (5-nights), transportation to-and-from airports and courses, carts, range, club storage, on-course beverages plus over $130,000 in combined prizes. To highlight the evenings, the tournament package also provides a Welcome Cocktail party, Mexican buffet and Awards Dinner during the tournament week. One -two nights are purposely left open allowing participants to experience Puerto Vallarta. The restaurants and night life of PV are unsurpassed. While competitive golf still remains the common link throughout the week, the Pro Am has evolved over 24 years to be as much about having fun as in winning.

The Pro Am provides team competition, professional competition(s), pro/scratch competitions and a multitude of specialty prizes over a 72-hole event. A special 36-hole spouse/companion tournament is also available.

As with the past, The 2013 Puerto Vallarta Pro Ams once again anticipates a full field (100 Teams). Registration for the 2013 Puerto Vallarta Pro Am will be online. Complete tournament information in printable formats is now available within our web site. A team spot may be reserved for a refundable $1000 deposit.

Vista Vallarta Club De Golf's reputation has grown whereas both the Nicklaus Signature and the Weiskopf Signature are among the highest rated in Latin America. The club is perched on the Sierra Madres overlooking Puerto Vallarta Vista boasts unspoiled terrain and spectacular views. Vista has hosted a World Golf Championship, a Champions Tour event and multiple other televised competitions. Experiencing both the Nicklaus and Weiskopf Signatures at Vista Vallarta may be the highlight to the Pro Am competition, but the 3rd course included in the tournament's rotation is the favorite of many. Marina Vallarta has stood the test of time. The course emphasizes shotmaking and strategy. Every hole offers a risk and reward. The lowest and the highest individual and team scores regularly come from the Marina course.

In keeping with the tradition established in 2001, $25.00 per player's entry fee will be used to perpetuate the Bill C. Sitton Sr. Scholarship Fund. The Fund has been and will continue to be managed by the Southern Texas PGA and provide education opportunities to a few in need. Thank you in advance for your support.
